Psychedelic Guide Training with Daniel McQueen

Dimensions Algonquin Highlands Clinical Director Donald Currie, RP, and Dawn Martin, PhD, unite with guest facilitator Daniel McQueen, MA, for a six-day, five-night training intensive.

Daniel McQueen, an international psychedelic educator and the founding director of the Center for Medicinal Mindfulness, is a globally recognized pioneer in the realm of psychedelicassisted therapy.

EVOLVE, is Dimensions’ inaugural practitioner retreat tailored to wellness clinicians and registered therapists with a solid foundation of personal development work, self-understanding, conscious evolution and ceremonial experiences. This private retreat is only open to an intimate group of invited participants and offers you the opportunity to continue your transformational journey while surrounded by others who are equally dedicated to becoming a cannabis-assisted psychedelic guide.

About Dimensions

Combining neuroscientific research with Plant Ceremony and luxurious hospitality, Dimensions focuses on restorative well-being and transformational growth. Dimensions Retreats help improve and transform lives by drawing on the restorative power of nature, spa rituals, remarkable cuisine and psychedelic Plant Ceremony in a growing collection of safe, legal and luxurious accommodations in natural settings.

Program

Retreat

Only 12 invited participants will be in attendance for our inaugural training Retreat at Dimensions Algonquin Highlands. Our lakeside Northern Ontario setting featuring rugged hills and an extensive network of forest trails is the optimal setting to observe the first signs of a Canadian spring. Time spent with us off the beaten path will serve as an exclusive prelude to Dimensions Education’s growing curriculum and the opening of a global collection of several permanent Retreat destinations for deep healing and well- being. You will learn why the combination of natural plant medicines and psychotherapeutic technique is a powerful strategy to address issues and reduce the impact of trauma – especially when healing is set in natural surroundings.

Program

Reset

EVOLVE is one of five well-being programs in Dimensions’ foundational repertoire. Your Retreat week with us will enrich your knowledge with relevant, in-depth training. Provide better care for your clients and feel more confident in the work you do as a therapist and wellness practitioner. Our leaders in the fields of psychotherapy, wellness and psychedelics will instruct you in best practices to ensure the highest levels of integrity, ethics and safety. You will gain new skills and receive certification in evidence-based psychedelic therapies, techniques and methods. We focus on mindfulness techniques that psychedelic therapists and guides can use to deepen their own healing and transformation process while assisting and staying in full contact with their client for many hours. These internal skill sets are often unacknowledged in other programs, yet are essential in increasing personal and professional sustainability and resilience.

Getting Here

Locations

45°6’22.48”N 78°39’29.55”W

Dimensions Algonquin Highlands can be reached from Toronto by car, helicopter or private air. Driving from Toronto via Highway 400 North with a connection to Highway 118 West brings guests to our door in approximately three hours. From Billy Bishop Toronto City Airport or Toronto Pearson International airport, flight time to the nearby Stanhope Municipal Airport (CND4) is one hour. The Retreat spans nearly 40 acres of woodlands and is located in Stanhope Township, along the shores of Maple Lake at 1218 Canopy Lane. This forest access road leads south off of the Township’s Airport Road, just 6km from the nearest community of Carnarvon and 20km from the larger town of Minden, Ontario.

The rate does not include ground transportation or airfare.
